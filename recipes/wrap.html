Wrap recipe

Description

This breakfast wrap will start your day off well!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bacon
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels, reserving grease in skillet.
  2. Add chicken to skillet and stir to coat with bacon drippings. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix garlic, salt, and pepper. Add tomato and onion to taste.
  4. Divide cooked bacon, chicken, and sauce mixture between lettuce leaves and wrap up.
