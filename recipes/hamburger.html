Hamburger Recipe

Description

This hamburger recipe is flavorful, juicy, and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Seasonings
  • Herbs & Spices

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make 4 patties
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center.
