Ramen Recipe

Description

This ramen tastes heavenly!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 package any flavor ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  2. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet until well blended.
